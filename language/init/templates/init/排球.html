本課程培養學生下列知識：
挑戰自我彈性與耐力性的運動，並且培養彼此默契的運動，兩隊(雙方)對抗，用手擊球過網以決勝負的球類運動。它不但是在球場上進行技術、體力、智力與心理等各方面的較量；其技術的層面對比賽結果的影響最為顯著，排球運動中的技術繁多，包括發球、傳接球、拳球、扣球、攔網、

2.低手發球基本技能訓練
3.上手發球基本技能訓練

5.綜合技能訓練

Course description: A sport with the challenge of self-elasticity and endurance. It is an exercise among 2 teams that scored by hitting the ball with hands cross the net; it develops the tacit agreement among teammates. Volleyball is a competition not only among the strategies, but also among physical strength, intelligence, and psychology. There are lots of volleyball strategies that have distinct influence on the result of a competition. Volleyball strategies include serving a ball, passing a ball, hitting a ball, knotting a ball, blocking, defense, etc.
